UX Nanodegree – Graduation Project (view Project here)

During my UX Nanodegree at Udacity, I worked on FitSphere, a mobile fitness app designed to help users set goals, track progress, and stay consistent in their health journey.

This project gave me the chance to lead the entire design process end-to-end, from research to prototyping.

Roles & Responsibilities

  • UX Research: Competitive analysis, Think-aloud studies, Accessibility audits.
  • UX Design: Wireframes, Prototypes, Case studies.
  • Product Handoff: Preparing designs for engineering handoff.

Collaboration

  • Guided by instructors and reviewed by Udacity mentors.

Tools Used

  • Figma
  • Lookback
  • Zeplin

Duration

4 Months

Key Outcomes

  • Learned to turn user insights into intuitive interfaces
  • Designed accessible and inclusive user experiences
  • Built a full UX case study with real feedback cycles
