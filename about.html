About me

Hi, I’m Sana Saleh, a UI/UX Designer from Palestine with a background in Software Engineering.

I’m passionate about designing intuitive and user-friendly digital experiences, whether for web or mobile applications.

Through my academic journey and internships, I’ve gained hands-on experience in user research, usability testing, wireframing, and prototyping using tools like Figma and Adobe XD.

I also enjoy bridging the gap between design and development, thanks to my knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

My goal is always to create designs that are both aesthetic and functional, enhancing how people interact with technology.

Outside of design, I love exploring productivity tools, traveling, and learning about new cultures, which continuously inspires my creative process.
